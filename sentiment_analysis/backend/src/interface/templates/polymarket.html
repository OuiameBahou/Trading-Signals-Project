How to read Crowd Intelligence signals
Bullish signal (Yes > 65%)

The crowd strongly believes the event will occur. When combined with positive news sentiment, this is a confluent bullish signal.

Bearish signal (Yes < 35%)

The crowd believes the event is unlikely. High-volume bearish markets indicate strong directional conviction in the prediction market.

Uncertain (35%–65%)

The market is genuinely split. Uncertainty in prediction markets often precedes high-volatility periods in underlying assets.

Signal Strength

Measures how far the probability is from 50% (maximum uncertainty). A strength of 1.0 = absolute certainty; 0.0 = coin flip.
